# 🧪 Lab 5 – Boost your productivity with data-driven decisions using Copilot

## 🎯 Objective

In this lab, participants learn how to use **Microsoft Copilot** to transform an email conversation into a fully scheduled meeting with minimal effort.

By the end of this lab, participants will be able to:
- Analyze the context of an email thread
- Identify participants automatically
- Check Outlook calendar availability
- Propose the best meeting time
- Generate a clear meeting subject and agenda

---

## 🧩 Scenario Context

You are part of an email discussion involving several stakeholders. A decision is required, and a meeting needs to be scheduled.

Instead of manually:
- Opening calendars
- Comparing availability
- Sending multiple follow‑up emails
- Writing the meeting agenda

You will rely on **Copilot** to handle the entire workflow.

---

## ✅ Prerequisites

- Microsoft 365 license with Copilot enabled
- Access to Outlook and Microsoft Teams
- An email thread with multiple participants

---

## 📧 Step 1 – Open the email thread

1. Open **Outlook**
2. Select an email thread that:
   - Includes multiple recipients
   - Clearly indicates the need for a discussion or decision

---

## 🤖 Step 2 – Use Copilot

Open Copilot in Outlook and enter the following prompt:

```text
Schedule a meeting for next week with all participants in this email thread.
Check attendees’ availability and propose the best available time slot.
Include a clear meeting subject and a brief agenda based on the context of this conversation.
```

---

## 🔍 Step 3 – What Copilot does

Copilot will automatically:

- Identify all participants from the email thread
- Search their Outlook calendars
- Detect common available time slots
- Propose the most optimal meeting time
- Generate:
  - A clear meeting subject
  - A concise agenda aligned with the conversation

> No manual calendar checks are required.

---

## 📊 Step 4 – Review the proposal

Before confirming, review:
- Proposed date and time
- Included participants
- Meeting subject clarity
- Agenda relevance

You can ask Copilot to:
- Adjust the duration
- Refine the subject
- Expand or simplify the agenda

---

## ✅ Step 5 – Confirm the meeting

Once satisfied:

1. Select **Confirm / Schedule meeting**
2. The meeting is added to all participants’ calendars
3. A Teams link is included automatically if applicable

---

## 🚀 Expected Outcome

Participants will learn how to:
- Turn email conversations into actionable meetings
- Reduce scheduling friction
- Save time on coordination tasks
- Improve meeting quality from the start

---

## 💡 Best Practices

- Use clear and contextual email threads
- Be specific in Copilot prompts
- Always review the proposal before confirming

---

## 🧠 Optional Extensions

- Create an urgent meeting scenario
- Add optional participants
- Ask Copilot to generate a post‑meeting summary
- Compare with manual scheduling

---

## ✅ Conclusion

Copilot streamlines everyday decision‑making by removing operational overhead.

Less time organizing — more time delivering impact.
