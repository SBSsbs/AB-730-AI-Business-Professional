# Boost Your Productivity with Data-Driven Decisions in Excel

## Lab Overview

In this lab, you act as a business manager using **Copilot in Excel** to turn data into actionable insights through natural language prompts. The focus is on **decision-making**, not manual Excel work.

You will analyze realistic business data covering:

* Client engagement trends
* Client feedback sentiment and themes
* Advisor performance and revenue

All data is fictional and used for training purposes only.

\---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lab, participants will be able to:

* Sort, filter, and highlight key data points
* Ask Copilot to calculate metrics and generate formulas
* Create charts and PivotTables
* Extract insights from text-based data
* Support business decisions using natural language prompts

\---

## Dataset Description

The dataset simulates data from a financial services and advisory organization (**Contoso**) and contains three worksheets:

### Sheet 1 – Monthly Trends

**Columns:**

* Month
* In-person Meetings
* Virtual Meetings
* Total Client Meetings
* Client Portal Engagement
* Online Searches for Financial Planning

### Sheet 2 – Client Feedback

**Columns:**

* Client ID
* Feedback Category
* Client Comment
* Sentiment (Positive, Neutral, Negative)

### Sheet 3 – Advisor Activity

**Columns:**

* Advisor Name
* Region
* Number of Client Meetings
* New Policies Sold
* Revenue Generated

## Part 1 – Analyze Trends and Summarize Data (Monthly Trends)

**Prompt 1**

```text
Summarize the main trends in client engagement across the months from the Monthly Trends sheet.
```

**Prompt 2**

```text
Generate a visual from these results to represent the trends.
```

**Prompt 3**

```text
Generate a new visual to represent the month over month percentage change for the total client meetings.
```

**Prompt 4**

```text
Using the Monthly Trends sheet, identify seasonality in total client meetings and explain what it implies for Contoso advisor capacity planning (staffing, outreach cadence, and appointment availability). Highlight the lowest and highest periods and recommend actions to smooth the late-summer dip and maximize the Q4 surge.
```

**Prompt 5**

```text
Analyze the in-person vs virtual meeting mix across months in the Monthly Trends sheet. For Contoso’s client experience strategy, quantify how dominant virtual is, identify months where the mix shifts, and suggest how to optimize the channel mix (e.g., when to push in-person reviews vs virtual touchpoints).
```
