# 🚀 AB-730 – AI Business Professional Certification Prep

Welcome to this repository dedicated to preparing for the **Microsoft AB-730: AI Business Professional** certification.

This repo is designed for **business users, consultants, and professionals** who want to leverage **Microsoft 365 Copilot and AI** to drive real business value — without writing code.

---

## 🎯 About the Certification

The **AB-730 certification** focuses on bridging the gap between **business needs** and **AI capabilities**.

Unlike technical certifications, it emphasizes:

* Business use cases
* Workflow optimization
* AI adoption strategy
* Responsible AI practices

🔗 Official Microsoft Learn page:
https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/credentials/certifications/ai-business-professional/

---

## 📚 What You Will Learn

This certification is structured around key skill areas:

### 1. Identify AI Opportunities

* Analyze business processes
* Detect automation potential
* Improve efficiency with AI

### 2. Define Practical Use Cases

* Translate business needs into AI solutions
* Solve real-world problems

### 3. Optimize Workflows with Copilot

* Use Microsoft 365 Copilot across apps
* Automate repetitive tasks

### 4. Leverage No-Code AI Tools

* Work with:

  * Microsoft Copilot
  * Researcher
  * Analyst
* Use tools in:

  * Word
  * Excel
  * PowerPoint
  * Teams

### 5. Drive Business ROI & Strategy

* Align AI with business goals
* Measure impact and value

### 6. Apply Responsible AI Principles

* Ensure:

  * Security
  * Privacy
  * Ethical AI usage
